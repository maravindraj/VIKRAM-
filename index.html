Tribute to Captain Vikram Batra

Captain Vikram Batra was a brave Indian Army officer who made the ultimate sacrifice during the **Kargil War of 1999**. His famous words **"Yeh Dil Maange More!"** became a symbol of courage and determination. Posthumously awarded the **Param Vir Chakra**, India's highest military honor, he remains an inspiration for generations.
